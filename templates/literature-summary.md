## Writing guidelines

Use this template to produce summaries that are useful for later hypothesis generation.

- Prefer plain language over compressed field-specific shorthand.
- Assume the reader has not read the original papers.
- Define specialized terms when necessary.
- Focus not only on what papers did, but also on what remains weakly supported, unresolved, or underexplored.
- Make gaps, tensions, and open questions explicit.
- Write with future hypothesis generation in mind.
